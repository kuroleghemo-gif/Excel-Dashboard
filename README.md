## Project Overview

##### Sales Performance Dashboard using Excel is a project designed to tackle the challenge of limited visibility into key business metrics for an online retail store. By leveraging Excel (pivot tables, charts, slicers) and dashboard design, it centralizes critical metrics and delivers actionable insights to support smarter business decisions. The dashboard aims to enhance sales performance, improve customer satisfaction, boost operational efficiency, and increase profitability.

## Project Objective

##### The primary objective of this project is to centralize key business metrics and deliver actionable insights that enable an online retail store to improve sales performance, enhance customer satisfaction, boost operational efficiency, and increase profitability.

## Data Source

##### The dataset used was provided by 10Alytics and contains a collection of features extracted from online retail store transactions, including the sales man, product, sale date, region, number of hours spent by sale, region, sales, sales per hour, revenue, calls and client satisfaction score. It comprises of 120 rows of sales recorded over 11 months.

## Data Preprocessing

##### The data used for this analysis was already cleaned and prepared, allowing the focus to be on generating actionable insights.

## Key Features

##### The Sales Performance Dashboard gives SwiftCart Hub a centralized view of sales, call activity, customer satisfaction, and profitability. Users can filter by region, product, or month, and pivot tables and charts allow trend analysis and performance comparisons. It highlights top-performing regions and sales repscustomer engagement, and operational efficiency.

## Key Insights

##### • Higher call activity is associated with increased sales, showing that customer engagement drives revenue.
##### • Germany is the top-performing region, contributing the highest sales among all regions.
##### • Sales performance varies across representatives, highlighting opportunities to replicate high-performing behaviors.
##### • Customer satisfaction is moderate, indicating room for improvement to enhance loyalty and retention.
##### • Centralized KPI tracking enables trend analysis, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions to optimize sales and operational efficiency.

## Conclusion

##### The Sales Performance Dashboard project successfully addresses the challenge of limited visibility into key business metrics for SwiftCart Hub, an online retail store. By centralizing data and leveraging Excel (pivot tables, charts, slicers) and dashboard design, the project delivers actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making. The dashboard highlights high-performing regions and sales representatives, demonstrates the impact of customer engagement on revenue, and provides a clear view of KPIs to improve sales performance, customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and profitability. This project demonstrates the value of turning data into strategic business insights.
